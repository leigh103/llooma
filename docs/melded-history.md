# Melded

## Development history
Melded was conceived in 2022 after a website design consultancy with Bury Music Service. Gemma, the head of service needed a replacement for Paritor - their previous admin software that was't working for them.

After working on a hair dresser booking system, Mike and Lee used their experience from that to build a new system from the ground up, dedicated to helping music services with their online booking and invoicing.
